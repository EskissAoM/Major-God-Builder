AoM:R Major God Builder Changelog

Version 1.1

Features:
- Favored Land related bonuses can now be used by all pantheons.
- Bushidō can now be used by all pantheons except Aztec.
- Favored Land does not produce Favor, and Bushidō is earned more slowly by non-Japanese pantheons, so Chinese and Japanese still make the strongest use of those tools while other pantheons gain more creative build options.

Fixes:
- Fixed Priests not being able to trigger Valley of the Kings tech.
- Fixed Sky Passage still costing Wood with Egyptian gods.
