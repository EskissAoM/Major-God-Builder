AoM:R Major God Builder

Create a custom major god by choosing a pantheon, god power, unique technology, bonuses, and minor gods, then export a ready-to-install mod ZIP.

1. Major god

Greek-only choices

Choose one hero for each age from that age's Greek hero pool, plus one Fortress unique unit.

Chinese-only choices

Choose one Mythic Age special hero.

Aztec-only choices

Choose one Classical Teixiptla Form and one Mythic Incarnate choice.

2. Unique technologies

Choose one major-god unique technology. The list is filtered by the selected pantheon, with special availability rules where needed.

3. God bonuses

Choose up to four major-god bonuses.

4. Minor gods

Choose exactly two minor gods for each age.

5. Export

How to install your mod

  1. Generate the mod ZIP.
  2. Unzip the folder.
  3. Go to C:\Users\UserName\Games\Age of Mythology Retold\SteamID\mods\local.
  4. Drop the unzipped mod folder there.
  5. Start the game and enjoy!

Quick changelog

Version 1.1

Features

  • Favored Land related bonuses can now be used by all pantheons.
  • Bushidō can now be used by all pantheons except Aztec.

I first wanted to keep some flavor for Japanese and Chinese by making those bonuses exclusive to them. Some players were theorycrafting around them, so I decided to let their full creativity bloom. Favored Land does not produce Favor and Bushidō is earned more slowly by other pantheons, meaning Chinese and Japanese will still make the strongest use of those tools.

Fixes

  • Fixed Priests not being able to trigger Valley of the Kings tech.
  • Fixed Sky Passage still costing Wood with Egyptian gods.

Updated

What’s new in version 1.1

  • Favored Land bonuses are now usable by all pantheons.
  • Bushidō is now usable by all pantheons except Aztec.
  • Priest / Valley of the Kings and Egyptian Sky Passage cost fixes.
