AoM Retold Major God Creator

This draft lets you choose a pantheon, any existing Archaic Age god power, and two existing minor gods per age, then generates a mod ZIP for a custom major god.

Draft status: this outputs major_gods_mods.xml, techtree_mods.xml, stringmods.txt, and GodPicker/TechTree XAML files. The pregame GodPicker and TechTree files use compact generated Archaic Age power blocks and real vanilla blocks for the selected minor gods. It is still a test generator: major-god-specific bonuses such as Hades shades will be handled later.
