AoM:R Major God Builder

Create a custom major god by choosing a pantheon, god power, unique technology, bonuses, and minor gods, then export a ready-to-install mod ZIP.

1. Major god

Greek-only choices

Choose one hero for each age from that age's Greek hero pool, plus one Fortress unique unit.

Chinese-only choices

Choose one Mythic Age special hero.

Aztec-only choices

Choose one Classical Teixiptla Form and one Mythic Incarnate choice.

2. Unique technologies

Choose one major-god unique technology. The list is filtered by the selected pantheon, with special availability rules where needed.

3. God bonuses

Choose up to four major-god bonuses.

4. Minor gods

Choose exactly two minor gods for each age.

5. Export

How to install your mod

  1. Generate the mod ZIP.
  2. Unzip the folder.
  3. Go to C:\Users\UserName\Games\Age of Mythology Retold\SteamID\mods\local.
  4. Drop the unzipped mod folder there.
  5. Start the game and enjoy!
